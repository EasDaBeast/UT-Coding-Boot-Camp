How to make a PB&J sandwich

  1. Get jars of peanut butter and jelly.
  2. Get 2 slices of bread.
  3. Scoop and smear a tablespoon of peanut butter onto the bread.
  4. Scoop and smear a tablespoon of jelly onto the other bread.
  5. Combine the two slices together.
